Alexandria Sofiakis Announces Candidacy for IL-10

February 16, 2026

By Campaign Staff

Share
Email

Alexandria Sofiakis, a longtime resident of Illinois' 10th Congressional District and a dedicated advocate for community empowerment, officially announced her candidacy for Congress today. Running on a platform centered around transparency, economic fairness, and grassroots engagement, Sofiakis said her campaign is committed to ensuring that everyday people—not corporations or special interests— have a voice in Washington.

Sofiakis has pledged that her campaign will not accept donations from corporate PACs or special interest groups. Instead, she plans to rely on the support of individual voters and small-dollar contributions from members of the community. According to Sofiakis, building a campaign funded by everyday people is essential to creating a government that truly represents them.

She has also stated that her campaign will reject funding from AIPAC and AIPAC-aligned organizations, emphasizing her commitment to maintaining an independent foreign policy rooted in diplomacy, human rights, and accountability. Sofiakis said she believes elected officials should remain free from outside influence when making decisions that affect both domestic and international policy, especially from influence that aligns with genocidal regimes or undermines human rights.

Even before formally entering the race, Sofiakis had already received the endorsement of Movement4Freedom, a grassroots organization dedicated to supporting candidates who prioritize social justice, civil liberties, and economic equality.

With her campaign now underway, Sofiakis says she looks forward to meeting voters across Illinois' 10th District and building a movement rooted in grassroots organizing, transparency, and accountability.
