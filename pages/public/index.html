Meet Alexandria Sofiakis

Alexandria Sofiakis is a dedicated public servant committed to making a positive impact in congress. She's not the typical corpo-politician; Unlike career politicians, Alexandria brings a fresh perspective and a genuine passion for serving her community. She also doesn't take corporate PAC money, ensuring her decisions are made with the best interests of her constituents in mind.

Her platform centers around key issues such as affordable healthcare, quality education, environmental sustainability, and economic development. Alexandria believes in the power of grassroots movements and is dedicated to fostering community engagement to create lasting change.
