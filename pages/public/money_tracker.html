Money Tracker

Transparency is essential to a healthy democracy. This Money Tracker allows voters to explore and compare campaign fundraising data for candidates running in this race.

All financial information displayed here is sourced directly from OpenFEC, a nonpartisan organization that aggregates and publishes data using publicly available filings submitted by campaigns and committees to the FEC. The data is updated as new reports are filed and reflects totals for each election cycle. The FEC publishes new reports daily, so figures may change over time.

Users can view individual candidates or compare two candidates side by side to better understand how campaigns are funded — including contributions from individual donors, political action committees (PACs), party committees, and other sources.

Alexandria Sofiakis believes voters deserve clear and accessible information about campaign finance. As reflected in the data, Alexandria has not accepted funding from corporate PACs, registered lobbyists, special interest groups, out-of-state-donors, or individual contributions. This tracker is provided to ensure accountability and to give voters the tools to make informed decisions.

Data source: OpenFEC. Figures are aggregated by election cycle and may change as amended or new filings are processed.
