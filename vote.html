Early In-Person Voting Information

Early voting in Illinois begins 40 days before Election Day. You can vote at any early voting location in your county. Make sure to bring a valid ID with you when you go to vote early.

  • When: Starts 40 days before Election Day
  • Where: Any early voting location in your county
  • ID Requirements: Valid government-issued ID

For more information on early voting locations and hours, visit the Illinois State Board of Elections website.
